About

Carrie Lorraine is a multidisciplinary artist and astrologer whose work blends intuitive insight with bold, emotional painting and cosmic digital design.

Through her Cosmic Logic Art brand, she explores patterns of love, identity, and human connection—translating cosmic systems into practical tools for self-understanding.

Her books and artwork invite readers to see the larger pattern behind their experiences and navigate life with clarity, curiosity, and depth.
